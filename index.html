What is good design?

I've realized that I really like color when it comes to design. Another thing that I like to see is the use of non-traditional mediums for advertising purposes, if that makes sense. For example, the Sweetgreen ad I posted is from a collaboration with an accessory brand called Susan Alexandra. Another example is the Sandy Liang shoe made out of knit. I think these are both super creative ways to draw attention to your brand. I also love design that is made to look 'vintage' or references a major part of pop culture. The Juliet Johnstone tank top ads are designed to look like older catalog magazines. They remind me of the teen magazines I would read as a kid or the catalogs I would look through at Christmas time. They're nostalgic.
